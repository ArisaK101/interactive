Ancient Scroll Tool 2
Drawer
Chest of drawers, type of furniture developed in the mid-17th century
from a chest with drawers in the base. By the 1680s the “chest” was
entirely made up of drawers: three long ones of varying depth, topped by
two short ones side by side.
Ancient Scroll Tool 3
Bamboo Mat
In 794-1185, there was the "sudare," or bamboo blind,
used to partition rooms at court. It is assumed that the
birth of makisu, a smaller version of the bamboo
screen, coincided with the appearance of nori maki
(seaweed rolls).
